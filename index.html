Our Process

From Sugarcane to Tableware

Our sustainable manufacturing process ensures minimal environmental impact at every stage

1

Sourcing Raw Bagasse

We collect sugarcane bagasse (the fibrous residue left after juice extraction) from local sugar mills. This agricultural byproduct would otherwise be burned as waste, but we give it new life as premium tableware.

2

Pulp Processing

The bagasse is cleaned, pulped, and mixed with water to create a fibrous slurry. Natural binders are added to strengthen the material. The entire process uses minimal water which is recycled in a closed-loop system.

3

Molding & Shaping

The pulp is pressed into molds under heat and pressure to form plates, bowls, and containers. Each piece is precisely shaped for optimal functionality and structural integrity.

4

Quality Control & Packaging

Each product undergoes rigorous quality checks before being packed in compostable or recyclable packaging. We ensure every shipment meets our high standards before reaching your business.
